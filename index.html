INGRESS PORTAL ROUTE PLANNER

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Click on a portal.
  2. Click on the link chain ().
  3. Copy the presented URL into the Portal Planner's Portal URLs field.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for each portal you wish to visit, pasting each URL on to a new line.
  5. Set your desired options and click "Generate Route".
  • Note that due to limitations of Google Maps, a maximum of 10 points may be used.
  • If you face any bugs or issues, feel free to raise an issue on GitHub.

Auto-optimise will automatically reorder your waypoints to provide the most efficient route. Note that this does not apply to the first and last waypoints.
